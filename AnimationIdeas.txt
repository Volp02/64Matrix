Animation Visual Design Plans
1. Liquid Simulation
Appearance: Fluid blob with surface tension, smooth edges, slight transparency
Motion: Flows and sloshes, responds to gravity, forms droplets
Colors: Blue/cyan gradient, brighter at edges, darker in center
Details: Ripples on surface, splashes on impact, smooth merging when blobs touch
3. Sand/Grains
Appearance: Individual grains (1-2 pixels), slightly varied colors
Motion: Falls straight down, piles up, forms slopes, avalanches when too steep
Colors: Beige/tan/yellow palette, slight color variation per grain
Details: Realistic pile physics, grains roll down slopes, forms natural mounds
4. Wave Interference
Appearance: Concentric ripples expanding from multiple sources
Motion: Waves expand, interfere (add/subtract), create patterns
Colors: Single color (cyan/blue), brightness = wave amplitude
Details: Interference patterns (peaks and troughs), waves pass through each other
6. Double Pendulum
Appearance: Two connected pendulums, simple lines/rods
Motion: Chaotic, unpredictable paths, sometimes loops, sometimes wild swings
Colors: Two colors (one per pendulum), trail showing path history
Details: Trail fades over time, shows the chaotic attractor pattern
7. Growing Plants
Appearance: Organic branching structures, starts as a seed
Motion: Grows upward, branches randomly, leaves appear on branches
Colors: Green palette, darker trunk, lighter leaves
Details: Natural branching angles, leaves sway slightly, growth is gradual
9. Rain
Appearance: Vertical streaks (1-2 pixels wide), varying lengths
Motion: Falls straight down at constant speed, splashes on ground
Colors: Light blue/cyan, slightly transparent
Details: Splash effect on impact (small circle that fades), varying drop sizes
10. Aurora
Appearance: Flowing, wavy bands of light across the screen
Motion: Slow horizontal drift, vertical waves, color shifts
Colors: Green, blue, purple, pink — flowing gradients
Details: Soft edges, color transitions, ethereal glow, waves move like curtains
12. Fire
Appearance: Flames with organic, flickering shapes
Motion: Rises upward, flickers randomly, occasional bursts
Colors: Red at base, orange/yellow in middle, white/yellow at tips
Details: Heat distortion effect, sparks, varying flame heights, natural randomness
13. Kaleidoscope
Appearance: Symmetric, mirrored patterns that rotate
Motion: Pattern rotates slowly, colors shift, new patterns emerge
Colors: Vibrant, high-saturation palette, multiple colors per pattern
Details: Perfect symmetry (4-8 fold), geometric shapes, smooth color transitions
14. Mandelbrot Zoom
Appearance: Fractal patterns with infinite detail
Motion: Continuously zooms into the fractal, revealing new detail
Colors: Color map based on iteration count (rainbow or palette)
Details: Smooth zoom, color cycling, mesmerizing detail, never-ending
18. Voronoi Cells
Appearance: Polygonal cells with distinct boundaries
Motion: Cells slowly morph as seed points move, boundaries shift
Colors: Each cell a different color from palette, smooth transitions
Details: Clean edges, cells pulse slightly, organic movement
20. Nebula
Appearance: Colorful, wispy clouds of gas
Motion: Slow drift and morph, gentle expansion
Colors: Deep purples, blues, pinks, oranges — cosmic palette
Details: Soft, blended edges, color gradients, ethereal glow, depth layers
21. Satellite Orbit
Appearance: Central planet (circle) with orbiting satellites
Motion: Satellites orbit in elliptical paths, trails show history
Colors: Planet in one color, satellites in different colors, trails fade
Details: Multiple satellites, varying orbit speeds, trail fades over time
22. Warp Speed
Appearance: Stars as points moving toward camera
Motion: Stars accelerate from center, create motion blur effect
Colors: White/yellow stars, blue streaks for speed lines
Details: Stars get larger as they approach, speed lines, tunnel effect
24. Snake Game
Appearance: Snake as connected segments, food as single pixel
Motion: Snake moves in grid, grows when eating, game over on collision
Colors: Snake in one color, food in contrasting color, background dark
Details: Classic gameplay, smooth movement, clear boundaries
25. Pong
Appearance: Two paddles (vertical bars) and a ball
Motion: Ball bounces between paddles and walls, paddles move up/down
Colors: Paddles and ball in contrasting colors, dark background
Details: Score display, smooth ball physics, responsive paddle movement
26. Pac-Man Maze
Appearance: Classic maze layout, Pac-Man as wedge, ghosts as blobs
Motion: Pac-Man moves through maze, ghosts chase, dots to collect
Colors: Yellow Pac-Man, colored ghosts, blue maze, white dots
Details: Classic gameplay, ghost AI patterns, power pellets, maze layout
28. Conway's Game of Life
Appearance: Grid of cells, alive = bright, dead = dark
Motion: Cells evolve based on rules, patterns emerge and die
Colors: Alive cells in bright color, dead cells fade to black
Details: Classic rules, interesting patterns (gliders, oscillators), smooth evolution
30. Plasma
Appearance: Flowing, organic patterns with smooth color gradients
Motion: Patterns flow and morph continuously, never repeating exactly
Colors: High-saturation gradients (rainbow or palette-based)
Details: Smooth color transitions, organic shapes, mesmerizing flow, no hard edges
32. Metaballs
Appearance: Blobby, organic shapes that merge when close
Motion: Balls move around, merge into larger blobs, separate when far
Colors: Single color or gradient, brighter at edges
Details: Smooth merging, organic shapes, soft edges, liquid-like behavior
33. Particle Trails
Appearance: Particles with glowing trails behind them
Motion: Particles move in patterns, trails fade over time
Colors: Bright particles, trails fade from bright to dark
Details: Long, smooth trails, particles in various patterns (spirals, orbits, etc.)
34. Lightning
Appearance: Jagged, branching bolts of electricity
Motion: Sudden flash, branches spread, fades quickly
Colors: Bright white/cyan core, purple/blue edges, intense brightness
Details: Random branching, multiple bolts, flash effect, quick fade
35. Snowfall
Appearance: Snowflakes as small white pixels or simple shapes
Motion: Falls slowly, drifts slightly side to side, accumulates on ground
Colors: White snowflakes, darker background, white accumulation
Details: Varying fall speeds, gentle drift, builds up at bottom, some flakes larger
36. Leaves
Appearance: Leaf shapes (simple triangles or ovals) in autumn colors
Motion: Falls with tumbling motion, drifts in wind, lands on ground
Colors: Red, orange, yellow, brown — autumn palette
Details: Tumbling rotation, wind effects, natural fall paths, accumulate on ground
37. Fireflies
Appearance: Small bright points that twinkle
Motion: Slow, gentle floating movement, random twinkling
Colors: Warm yellow/white, bright when lit, dim when off
Details: Random on/off twinkling, gentle drift, creates magical atmosphere
38. Stars
Appearance: Twinkling points of light scattered across screen
Motion: Stars twinkle (brightness varies), some drift slowly
Colors: White/yellow stars, varying brightness, dark blue/black sky
Details: Random twinkling patterns, some stars brighter, creates starfield effect
